Cafe Afterlife

Projection Mapping Installation, Nov 1, 2019

This public art installation was presented in collaboration with the SJSU Hammer Theatre and SJSU CADRE Student Organization student club. This installation was presented on Nov 1st, 2019 at the Hammer Theatre Cafe, located in downtown San Jose, as a part of the local First Friday Festivies.The installation was inspired by the theme of halloween nostalgia and combining the aesthetic of the festival of the Day of the Dead, DÃ­a de Muertos, being Nov 1st. During the project timeline, I was the active President of this student organization ran various various workshops and tutorials of fabrication and software techniques that goes into a projection mapping installation.

The project had a mix of practical and digital effects, such as smoke machines, mannequins, live-action filming, rotoscoping animation, 3D virtual environments, digital image and video editing, and physical electronics that which was projected around the space.
